Who We Are

A Place to
Belong, Grow,
and Serve

Second Church COCHUSA is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community of believers committed to Evangelism, witnessing for Christ by seeking the lost and unchurched.

Whether you are new to faith or have walked with Christ for decades, you will find a home here. We believe the church is most powerful when every member is equipped and empowered to live out their calling.

Our ministries are made excellent through skillful stewardship of our talents, time, facilities, finances, and all resources entrusted to us by god and his people, at every level of the church.

Community Life

Discovering Self
Through Personal
Prayer & Fellowship

At Second Church COCHUSA, we believe community transforms. When believers come together — sharing meals, praying for one another, celebrating milestones and bearing burdens — we become the living Body of Christ.

Our community is marked by warmth and authenticity. No one walks alone here. From our newest first-time visitor to our longest-tenured member, everyone is seen, known, and loved.
